About

The purpose of this site is to encourage conscious decisions about how one uses the time and money spent commuting. We are granted 24 hours per day for a limited time on earth (e.g., 80 years, 80 * 365 * 24 h to put it bluntly), so being aware of how this time is spent is important. Calculating the true cost of commuting can facilitate empowering choices. Whether this time is spent to unwind and mode-switch, or sit frustrated in a car queue, can make a large difference over the long run.

This website is inspired by Mr. Money Mustache's writing on this topic, especially The True Cost of Commuting. CommutingValue is meant as a practical tool to help people calculate this in a fast, clear, and shareable way.

CommutingValue exists to support deliberate, long-horizon decisions about time.
